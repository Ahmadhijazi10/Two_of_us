The iPhone 16, Apple's latest flagship, boasts a sleek design with a wider color palette, a powerful A18 Bionic chip, and an improved dual-lens camera system with a 48MP main sensor and 2x optical zoom. Its 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display supports HDR10 and Dolby Vision, while the larger battery offers up to 22 hours of video playback. Additional features include Wi-Fi 7, a USB-C port, an Action button, Spatial audio, and Emergency SOS via satellite.
